About Us

We are traditional spice growers from the spice hills of IDUKKI which is located in the western ghats of India.

Our Ancestry

Our ancestors have migrated from different parts of Kerala to Idukki and became the experts in the production and processing of high-yield wide varieties of spices.

Legacy Farms

Now our farms are nearly a century years old, and our expertise too.

Industry Leaders

Through our hard work and dedication, we have become one of the largest suppliers of processed green cardamom across India, with a processing capacity of 20 tons per day.
